## Classification Guide

**Category A — Accepts without negotiation**
- Direct acceptance
- No questions about price
- No hesitation
- Language indicates willingness to pay
- Examples: "Yes", "Absolutely", "I'd pay that", "Sign me up"

**Category B — Accepts with questions**
- Accepts but asks questions
- May negotiate or ask for clarification
- Still indicates willingness but with conditions
- Examples: "Yes, but...", "Can I pay annually?", "What happens if...?"

**Category C — Declines or hesitates**
- Declines outright
- Expresses hesitation
- Questions value at that price
- May express loss of trust
- Examples: "That's too much", "I can't afford that", "I'm not sure"

**Critical Signal:**
- [ ] 0 users express loss of trust (if any user expresses loss of trust, threshold fails)
