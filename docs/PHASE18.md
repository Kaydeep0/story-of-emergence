**Status:** Agency Boundary Declaration

## Goal

Explicitly declare that all insight, synthesis, prioritization, and action arise from the human observer, not the system.

This phase prevents users from attributing agency, wisdom, or authority to the system itself.

---

## Core Principle

**The system has no intent.  
The system has no goals.  
The system does not "help."**

**The system exposes structure.  
The observer assigns meaning.**

---

## 1. Agency Disclaimer

**Declare that the system:**

### Reasoning and Outcomes

1. **Does not reason toward outcomes**
   - The system does not reason, think, or process information
   - The system does not work toward any outcome or result
   - The system does not have goals or objectives
   - The system computes and displays, nothing more

2. **Does not optimize for improvement**
   - The system does not optimize anything
   - The system does not work toward improvement or betterment
   - The system does not have preferences for "better" states
   - The system presents structure as it exists

### Guidance and Suggestions

3. **Does not suggest next steps**
   - The system does not suggest, recommend, or guide
   - The system does not propose actions or directions
   - The system does not offer advice or counsel
   - The system presents data, nothing more

4. **Does not hold preferences**
   - The system does not prefer one structure over another
   - The system does not value certain patterns over others
   - The system does not have opinions or judgments
   - The system treats all structures equally

### Understanding and Knowledge

5. **Does not possess understanding**
   - The system does not understand what it displays
   - The system does not comprehend meaning or significance
   - The system does not have knowledge in a human sense
   - The system processes and presents, without understanding

### Agency Principles

- **The system has no agency**: It does not act, decide, or choose
- **The system has no intent**: It does not work toward any purpose
- **The system has no goals**: It does not optimize or improve
- **The system has no preferences**: It does not value or judge

---

## 2. Insight Ownership Statement

**Explicitly state:**

### All Insights Are Generated by the Observer

1. **All insights are generated by the observer**
   - Any insight, realization, or understanding originates in the human observer
   - The system does not generate insights
   - The system does not produce meaning or understanding
   - Insights arise from the observer's interpretation of structure

2. **Any perceived "realization" originates in the human**
   - If an observer has a realization while viewing the system, that realization is theirs
   - The system did not cause, create, or generate the realization
   - The system only presented structure; the observer interpreted it
   - All meaning-making occurs in the observer, not the system

3. **The system cannot be credited with insight production**
   - The system cannot be said to have "helped" generate insights
   - The system cannot be credited with "revealing" meaning
   - The system cannot be described as "insightful" or "wise"
   - The system is a tool, not a source of insight

### Insight Ownership Principles

- **Insights belong to the observer**: All meaning-making is human
- **The system enables, does not create**: Structure is presented, meaning is assigned
- **No credit to the system**: The system cannot be credited with insight production
- **Observer is the source**: All insights originate in the human observer

### Interpretation

   - The system does not share responsibility for prioritization

### Meaning Assignment

   - The system does not share responsibility for meaning
   - All meaning assignment is the observer's responsibility

### Action Decisions

   - The system does not suggest or recommend actions
   - The system does not share responsibility for decisions
   - All action decisions are the observer's responsibility

### Consequences

   - The system does not share responsibility for outcomes

### Responsibility Principles

- **No delegation to the system**: The observer cannot delegate responsibility to the system
- **No shared agency**: The system and observer do not share agency or responsibility
- **Observer is solely responsible**: All interpretation, prioritization, meaning, action, and consequences are the observer's responsibility

## 5. Forward Constraint

**Any future feature implying system agency violates the Mirror role.**

### Violation Criteria

Any feature that:
- Implies the system reasons, thinks, or processes information toward outcomes
- Suggests the system optimizes, improves, or works toward goals
- Indicates the system suggests, recommends, or guides
- Implies the system has preferences, values, or judgments
- Suggests the system understands, knows, or comprehends
- Uses anthropomorphic language (knows, understands, learns, reflects, guides, advises, helps, suggests, reveals, identifies)

**Constitutes a violation of the Mirror role declared in Phase 12.1 and the agency boundary established in this phase.**

### Violation Consequences

**Violations require rollback and explicit acknowledgment.**

If a violation occurs:
1. **Immediate rollback**: The violating feature must be removed
2. **Explicit acknowledgment**: The violation must be explicitly acknowledged in documentation
3. **Boundary reinforcement**: The agency boundary must be reinforced
4. **Prevention measures**: Measures must be taken to prevent future violations

### Forward Constraint Principles

- **No system agency**: All future features must respect the agency boundary
- **No anthropomorphism**: All future language must avoid anthropomorphic framing
- **Observer agency only**: All insight, meaning, and action remain with the observer
- **Violations are breaches**: Any violation is a breach of the Mirror role

## Agency Boundary Summary

**Agency disclaimer:** The system does not reason toward outcomes, optimize for improvement, suggest next steps, hold preferences, or possess understanding.

**Insight ownership:** All insights are generated by the observer. Any perceived "realization" originates in the human. The system cannot be credited with insight production.

**Responsibility transfer:** The observer alone is responsible for interpretation, prioritization, meaning assignment, action decisions, and consequences. The system cannot share or absorb responsibility.

**Prohibited anthropomorphism:** Language implying the system knows, understands, learns, reflects, guides, advises, helps, suggests, reveals, or identifies is explicitly forbidden.

**Forward constraint:** Any future feature implying system agency violates the Mirror role. Violations require rollback and explicit acknowledgment.
